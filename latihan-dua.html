Shakespeare's Sonnet #18

This is one of the most famous sonnets. It is referenced in the film Dead Poets Society and gave names to the band The Darling Buds and the book and television series The Darling Buds of May. Read it and weep!

  • I compare thee to summer's Day?
  • tou art more lovely and more temperate:
  • rough winds do shake the darling buds of May
  • and summer's lease hath all too short a date:
  • sometime too hot the eye of heaven shines
